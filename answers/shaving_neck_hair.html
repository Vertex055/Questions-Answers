Ruling on Shaving Neck Hair

Question: Is it permissible to shave the neck beard, as it is not considered part of the beard?

Answer: In the name of Allah, all praise is due to Allah, and may Peace and Blessings be upon the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ).

The hair on the neck is not considered from the beard so there is no problem in shaving it.

And Allah knows best.
